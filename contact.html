Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline depends on the project scope and complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex applications can take 2-3 months. I provide a detailed timeline after our initial discussion.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance plans to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. This includes regular backups, security monitoring, and content updates if needed.

What is your payment process?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, I work with clients from all over the world. I use various communication tools like Zoom, Slack, and email to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location.

What technologies do you work with?

I work with a wide range of technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Node.js, MongoDB, PHP, WordPress, and more. I choose the best technology stack for each project's specific needs.

Can you help with existing websites?

Absolutely! I can help with website redesigns, performance optimization, adding new features, or fixing issues with existing websites. Just provide details about your current setup.
